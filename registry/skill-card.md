# ShipVitals

**Category:** Release readiness

**Description:** Audit command, runtime, visual, security, and delivery evidence before publishing or delivering a project.

**Users:** Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, OpenCode, Lovable, Bolt, Replit, and Windsurf users; freelancers; solo builders; small agencies.

**Outputs:** `READY`, `ALMOST READY`, `NOT READY`, or `DEMO ONLY`, with evidence and retest commands.
