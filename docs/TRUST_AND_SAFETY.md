# Trust and Safety

ShipVitals is designed to reduce false confidence in AI-assisted projects.

## Risks it checks

- exposed secrets;
- fake integrations;
- placeholder UI;
- skipped tests;
- silent failure handlers;
- broken links;
- missing privacy/support handoff;
- mobile/visual proof gaps;
- overconfident READY verdicts without evidence.

## Risks it does not fully solve

- professional pentesting;
- regulated compliance;
- legal review;
- accessibility certification;
- security research against hostile users.

When stakes are high, ShipVitals must trigger specialist chaining.
